# Brand & Content Guidelines

## Company Identity

**zatsit** is a French tech consulting company based in Lille (Euratechnopole).

### Mission
> Engager notre expertise numérique au service de l'impact des entreprises, en créant un écosystème durable, partenarial et positif.

### Values
- **Impact** - Technology at the service of positive change
- **Expertise** - Software craftsmanship, clean code, best practices
- **Transparency** - Shared governance, open collaboration
- **Sustainability** - Eco-design, responsible tech
- **Partnership** - Clients as partners, consultants at the heart of strategy

## Brand Voice

### Tone
- Professional but approachable
- Confident without being arrogant
- Technical but accessible
- Human and authentic

### Language
- Website content: **French**
- No corporate jargon
- Clear and concise
- Action-oriented

## Content Guidelines

### Headlines
- Use gradient text for key words (`<span class="text-gradient">impact</span>`)
- Keep headlines short and impactful
- Focus on value proposition

### Body Text
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Use bullet points for lists
- Highlight key terms with `<strong>`

### Call-to-Actions
- Clear and action-oriented
- Use primary button style (`.btn-primary`)
- Examples: "Contactez-nous", "Découvre ton futur package", "Travaillons ensemble"

## Visual Identity

### Colors
- **Light theme**: Blue (#0f15fd) - Professional, tech
- **Dark theme**: Gold/Orange (#f1be51) - Warm, impactful

### Logo
- Use `Logo.astro` component
- Always in primary color (adapts to theme)

### Icons
- Outline style preferred
- Use `currentColor` for theme adaptation
- Consistent sizing (`.icon-sm`, `.icon-md`, `.icon-lg`)

## Key Messages

### For Clients
- Experienced and passionate consultants
- Strategic partner for technology challenges
- Focus on impact and innovation

### For Consultants
- Choose your status (employee, freelance, portage)
- Valued contributions
- Shared governance
- Collective expertise

### Eco-responsibility
- Eco-designed websites
- EcoVadis Silver certified
- Partners: Framework, Ekip, BCorp orientation
